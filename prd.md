## Product Requirements Document (PRD)
### Chatbot for JO's Bike Shop

---

### 1. Overview
A text-based chatbot designed to assist customers visiting the JO's Bike Shop website. The chatbot aims to provide quick, helpful, and friendly support for common customer needs.

### 2. Objectives & Goals
- Improve customer experience and engagement
- Reduce manual workload for staff
- Provide instant answers to common questions
- Guide users to products and services

### 3. Functional Requirements

#### Single-Turn Capabilities
- Answer shop hours, location, and contact info
- Show available bikes and accessories
- Check product availability
- Provide maintenance tips
- Handle FAQs (returns, warranties, delivery)
- Guide users to online purchase or reservation
- Collect customer feedback

#### Multi-Turn Capabilities
- **Book Service Appointments** - Intelligently collects required information through conversation:
  - Required: Service type, preferred date, preferred time
  - Optional: Bike details, specific issues, contact information
  - Handles both single-turn (all details provided upfront) and multi-turn flows (asks follow-up questions as needed)
  - Confirms all collected details before finalising

### 4. Non-Functional Requirements
- Fast response times
- Easy to update shop name and details
- Secure handling of customer data
- Scalable for increased traffic

### 5. User Stories / Use Cases
- As a customer, I want to know the shop's opening hours.
- As a customer, I want to see which bikes are available.
- As a customer, I want to book a service appointment.
- As a customer, I want to ask about returns and warranties.
- As a customer, I want to get tips for bike maintenance.

### 6. Success Metrics
- % of customer queries resolved by chatbot
- Customer satisfaction ratings
- Reduction in manual support requests
- Number of bookings made via chatbot

### 7. Technical Considerations
- Platform: Web-based, text interface
- Integration with shop inventory and booking system
- Support for future expansion (e.g., voice, multi-language)

### 8. Future Enhancements
 Enable personalised recommendations
